We are always looking for motivated and curious students who are interested in working with us on theses, research projects, or internships.

If you are excited about topics such as machine learning, medical image analysis, or trustworthy AI, we would be happy to hear from you. Students at all levels are welcome to reach out and explore possible opportunities within our group.

Below you can find a list of currently available topics. However, if none of these directly match your interests, please do not hesitate to contact us anyway. We strongly encourage initiative applications and are always open to discussing your own ideas or project proposals.

We look forward to hearing from you!
